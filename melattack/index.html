MelAttack: An Audio Adversarial Attack on Mel spectrogram

Abstract

Recently, the adversarial attacks on audio have received increasing attention. Neverthe-less, the current methods primarily focus on perturbations in the time domain, leaving the fre-quency-domain perturbations largely unexplored. To address this gap, we introduce a novel ap-proach called MelAttack, which implements adversarial attacks by modifying the Mel-spectrogram of the audios. Experiments evaluation conducted on ECAPA and ResNet, advanced speaker recognition models, demonstrate that our method outperforms those state-of-the-art methods in terms of both attack success rate and auditory quality. Furthermore, our method ex-hibits good robustness when confronted with various defense mechanisms.
